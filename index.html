My Story

Where ecological knowledge meets land economics

I am a Certified Master Naturalist, former HR and operations leader, wildlife gardening educator, and Garden Club President with a B.S. in Technical Management. But more than any title — I am proof that a small piece of land, tended with intention and knowledge, can become a complete income ecosystem.

For years I worked at the intersection of people, systems, and strategy in corporate environments. I know how to build frameworks that work, how to train others to use them, and how to lead organizations through complex change. What I discovered is that the same skills that make a great HR leader make a great land educator — clarity, systems thinking, and genuine care for the person in front of you.

Now I bring all of that to one mission: helping passionate gardeners, small land owners, and nature content creators understand that the land beneath their feet is the most powerful financial tool they will ever own.
